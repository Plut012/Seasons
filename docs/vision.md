# Turncoats Online

An online adaptation of Turncoats (by Matilda Simonsson) — a minimalist, moody web UI for playing with friends asynchronously.

## What It Is

- 2-5 player area control game with hidden loyalty
- 3 factions (black, blue, red) compete for territory on a shared map
- Core tension: playing stones from your hand helps a faction dominate the board, but you WIN by holding stones of the winning faction — every stone played is a stone you can't score with

## Who It's For

- Board game groups who want to play Turncoats remotely
- Async-friendly: take your turn whenever, get notified when it's yours

## Aesthetic Direction

- Muted, moody dark palette — medieval-but-minimal
- Smooth, organic SVG animations and flowing map lines
- Atmospheric without being heavy — think parchment-in-shadow, not fantasy-overload
- Desktop-first layout, mobile-aware

## Faction Colors

| Faction | Color | Hex (approx) | Feel |
|---------|-------|--------------|------|
| Green | Dark muted green | `#2d6a4f` | Forest, earth, old growth |
| Blue | Bright blue | `#4895ef` | Sky, water, clarity |
| Purple | Dark purple | `#7b2d8e` | Royalty, shadow, ambition |

Background and chrome should be dark/muted (charcoal, slate, near-black) so faction colors pop without being garish.

## Non-Goals

- Matchmaking / rankings / leaderboards
- User accounts or persistent profiles
- AI opponents
- Multiple maps (single official map for now)
- Mobile-first design
- Real-time competitive (async is the priority)
